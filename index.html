The Restaurant est.1983

Lose yourself within the endless blue of the Ionian sea, enjoy unique new flavors consisting of the freshest ingredients and become a part of a warm family that will offer you unforgetable moments and exceptional meals. This is Stani Taverna. Being the first restaurant in the area of Kalamaki we always acted motivated by our love for good food, the principles of greek hospitality as well as respect for our costumers. In the long run, the experience that we have gained allowed us to realise our vision which combines the greek traditional cuisine with snappy and friendly service.

Special Ocations

Plenty of open space, beautiful views and the cosy atmospere make our restaurant ideal for hosting a plethora of special events. Trust us to make them even more special!

Weddings - Anniversaries - Birthdays

The Bar

If you happen to prefer a drink, or have just finished your meal and it's time to relax, you can visit Papa Stani's Bar which although part of the restaurant, distinguishes itself by offering a different atmoshpere and of course your favourite drinks ranging from juices or soft drinks to exotic coctails and beers. You can also expect varied music that adds to the overall experience.

The Beach

Stani's Beach is one of the cleanest and beautiful beaches of the island. It is particularly suited for those looking to relax and enjoy crystal clear waters as well as view of Marathonissi island. Furthermore Stani's Beach is home to the caretta-caretta turtle, a rare turtle species that visitors admire every year. Best of all, the beach is only a step away from Stani Taverna and Bar.
